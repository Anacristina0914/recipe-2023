Procedure:
1. Put water to boil. Once it is boled add salt until it is salty enough.
2. Add pasta to water and cook as stated in the instructions.
3. Grate pecorino, approxmately 1 cup for every 100g of pasta.
4. Add the egg to the pecorino and mix until a paste is formed.
5. Add some of the water in which pasta is being cooked, slowly until cheese mix has a cream consistency.
6. Strain the pasta and add to cream.
7. Add pepper.
